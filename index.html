About

Hey! I'm Bartley, or Bart, It's strange to say my own full first name. Instead of writing some 3rd person biography about myself, I'll just simply tell you a few things about me and what I love to do.

I was born and raised in this shitty great city of Los Angeles. I knew from a young age that I wanted to make movies - rather, tell stories. I've been fascinated by tall tales and characters ever since I was a little boy and my parents would read me R.L. Stein's 'Goosebumps' before bed. That naturally evolved into a love of cinema and actors. I thought I wanted to be an actor so I studied professionally for 2 years, but that quickly morphed into a desire to be behind the camera and working WITH actors as opposed to being one. I began writing and shooting my own stories at the age of 13 and a few years later starting a company called C-41 Productions./ Under that label I wrote, produced, and directed short films, commercials, music videos, and internet content for creative clients. Now starving operating as a freelance director, I am constantly looking to expand my horizons as a creative force with in the industry.

When I'm not wracking my brain with how to stay relevant and getting another gig, I'm usually outside with my dogs, having brunch with my friends on the weekends, snowboarding, traveling, and of course always watching the latest cinematic creations of others, life begins at the end of your comfort zone.
